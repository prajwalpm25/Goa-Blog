DAY 1

The day starts at Calangute beach, where we, as a gang of four members, are out to explore North Goa for the first time. As most tourists do to explore Goa by rental, we did the same and rented a two-wheeler for our journey. Our journey begins at Anjuna Beach, which is 6.6 km away from Calangute Beach. Having fun on the beach, we then began to move to our next destination, which is Vagator beach. Vagator beach is 4 km away from Anjuna beach. After spending some time enjoying the beauty of nature near the shore, we began to move to our next destination, Chapora Fort. Chapora fort is close to Vagator beach, which can be reached in five to ten minutes. The Chapora fort is located at the top of the hill, so we need to climb the steps to reach it. The view from the fort is spectacular to watch. After experiencing fresh air, we then moved to our next destination, which is Parra Road. Parra Road is 7km away from Chapra Fort. This is one of the scenic spots where a scene from the Indian movie "Zindagi Na Milegi Dobara" was shot. We took a lot of pictures and headed towards our next destination, which is Baga beach. Baga beach is 6 km away from Perra Road. It is one of the most popular beaches in Goa.

DAY 3

The final day of our journey begins from Calangute beach to Sinquerium, which is 7 km away from Calangute beach. After spending some time at the lower Aguada fort, we then began to move to our next destination, Aguada fort. It is 4 km from the lower Aguada fort. After spending some time at the fort, we then moved to our next destination, Reis Magos fort, which is 10km away from Aguada fort. We saw canons mounted over the forts.
